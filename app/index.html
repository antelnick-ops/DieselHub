What’s a core charge?

A core charge is a refundable deposit added to certain rebuildable parts — alternators, starters, injectors, turbos, transmissions, and engine components.

How it works:

  • You pay the core deposit when you place your order.
  • After you install the new part, box up your old (broken) part.
  • Ship the old part back to us.
  • We refund your core charge once we receive it.

Tips:

  • You have up to 30 days to return the core.
  • The old part must be complete and rebuildable (no missing pieces).
  • Reuse the shipping box the new part came in.

Have questions? Contact us before ordering if you’re unsure about core requirements.
